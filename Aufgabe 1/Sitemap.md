**Sitemap and User Rights**

├── **Welcome** (guests)
├── **Dashboard** (user)
├── **Jobs** (user)
│   ├── **List View** (user)
│   ├── **Detail View** (user)
│   ├── **Edit View** (job owner)
│   ├── **Create View** (recruiter)
├── **Companies** (user)
│   ├── **List View** (user)
│   ├── **Detail View** (user)
│   ├── **Edit View** (company owner)
│   ├── **Create View** (user)
├── **Categories** (user)
│   ├── **List View** (user)
│   ├── **Detail View** (user)
│   ├── **Edit View** (admin)
│   ├── **Create View** (admin)
├── **Profile**
│   ├── **Edit View** (user)
│   ├── **Create View** (guests)

**Note:** Admin inherits the rights of:

- User
- Recruiter
- Job owner
- Company owner
